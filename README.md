# CareerConnect Pro

CareerConnect Pro is a modern Job Portal and Recruitment Management Platform that connects Job Seekers, Recruiters, and Administrators through a centralized hiring ecosystem. The platform enables job posting, job applications, resume management, interview scheduling, subscription plans, AI-powered career assistance, notifications, messaging, and analytics.

## Features

### Job Seeker

* User Registration & Authentication (JWT)
* Profile Management
* Resume Upload 
* Skill, Education & Experience 
* Job Search & Filtering
* Save Jobs
* Apply for Jobs
* Track Application Status
* Interview Notifications
* AI Career Assistant
* Subscription Management

### Recruiter

* Company Profile Management
* Recruiter Profile Management
* Create & Manage Job Postings
* View Candidates
* Review Applications
* Schedule Interviews
* Send Messages to Candidates
* Manage Hiring Pipeline
